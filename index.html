Our Story

We are on a mission to #Solarify 1 lakh rooftops by the year 2023.

In 2016, a young engineer, began his journey of – providing people easy access to solar power. He took the leap towards realising his vision by starting Solarify.

One person with a dream so big? In a sector dominated by giants like TATA and Adani, the challenges of starting up made the dream even bigger – with everything to play for.

Not just another start-up on the block.

  • With a combined experience of 10 years in social development and sustainability, we possess unique actionable insights about the market.
  • Our culture is built on human centred design thinking and that helps us understand you better than anyone else - yes, quite literally - and find solutions that suit you best.
  • We keep things simple for you, stay reliable (and available) for you, while making your investment in solar energy profitable.
